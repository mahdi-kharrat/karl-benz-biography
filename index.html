German engine designer and automotive engineer

Bertha Benz with her husband Karl Benz in a Benz Victoria, model 1894

Here's a time line of Dr. Borlaug's life:

  • 1844-Date of Birth.
  • 1860-Joins Karlsruhe University.
  • 1864-Graduates with an Engineering Major at Age 19.
  • 1871-Starts To Work In Mannheim.
  • 1872-Marriage.
  • 1887-The Final Model of The Motorwagen. First Vehicle on Earth.
  • 1883-The Partnership and Benz & Cie.
  • 1886-Motor Wagen is Patented.
  • 1879-The Combustion Engine and Patents that Would Change The World.
  • 1889-The MotorWagen is introduced to the planet.
  • 1896-Expansion.
  • 1899-Biggest Automobile Company in The World.
  • 1900-Competition.
  • 1926-World War and An Unexpected Alliance.
  • 1927-Sucess.
  • 1928-First Mercedes-Benz Car.
  • 1929-Death
